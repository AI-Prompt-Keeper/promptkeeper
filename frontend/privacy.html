Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Prompt Keeper (“we,” “us,” or “our”) collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our website and services.

1. Data We Collect

We may collect:

  • Account and profile data: email address, name, password (hashed), and preferences you provide when signing up or managing your account.
  • Service data: prompts, API keys (stored in encrypted form), and usage data (e.g., request counts, feature usage) necessary to operate and improve the Service.
  • Technical and log data: IP address, browser type, device information, and logs of requests and errors.
  • Communications: messages you send to us (e.g., support or feedback).

2. How We Use Your Data

We use the data we collect to:

  • Provide, operate, and improve the Service.
  • Authenticate you and enforce our Terms of Service.
  • Send service-related and, where permitted, marketing communications.
  • Comply with legal obligations and protect our rights and safety.

3. Sharing of Data

We do not sell your personal data. We may share data with:

  • Service providers: hosting, analytics, and support providers that process data on our behalf under contractual obligations.
  • LLM and API providers: when you use the proxy to run prompts, we send the necessary data (e.g., prompt content) to the provider you have configured; their privacy policies apply to that processing.
  • Legal and safety: when required by law or to protect rights, safety, or property.

4. Security

We use industry-standard measures to protect your data, including encryption at rest and in transit. See our Security Whitepaper for more detail.

5. Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the Service, comply with law, and resolve disputes. You may request deletion of your account and associated data subject to our retention policies and legal obligations.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to access, correct, delete, or port your data, or to object to or restrict certain processing. EU/EEA and UK users have additional rights under GDPR. Contact us at privacy@promptkeeper.example to exercise these rights. You may also have the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority.

7. Cookies and Similar Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies for session management, security, and analytics. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

8. Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post the revised policy and update the “Last updated” date. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.

9. Contact

For privacy-related questions or requests: privacy@promptkeeper.example. For EU/EEA data protection matters, see our GDPR & DPA page.
